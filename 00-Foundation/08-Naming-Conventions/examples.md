# Naming Convention Examples

All examples follow the pattern defined in `naming-convention.md`.

## Resource Groups
1. `rg-prod-hr-frc-001` – Production, HR app, France Central
2. `rg-prod-hr-eus-001` – Production, HR app, East US (disaster recovery)
3. `rg-dev-portal-weu-001` – Development, Company Portal, West Europe
4. `rg-sbx-ai-frc-001` – Sandbox, AI experimentation, France Central

## Virtual Machines
5. `vm-prod-hr-001` – First production VM for HR app
6. `vm-prod-hr-002` – Second production VM (horizontal scale)
7. `vm-dev-portal-001` – Development VM for portal team
8. `vm-sbx-ai-001` – Sandbox VM for AI testing

## Storage Accounts
9. `stprodhrfrc001` – Production storage, HR app, France Central
10. `stdevportalweu001` – Development storage, Portal app, West Europe
11. `stsbxaifrc001` – Sandbox storage, AI experiments, France Central
12. `stprodhrfrc002` – Secondary storage account for logs in same region

## Virtual Networks
13. `vnet-prod-hr-frc-001` – Production HR network, France Central
14. `vnet-dev-portal-weu-001` – Development portal network
15. `vnet-sbx-ai-frc-001` – Sandbox AI network

## Network Security Groups
16. `nsg-prod-hr-frc-001` – NSG for production HR subnet
17. `nsg-dev-portal-weu-001` – NSG for development portal subnet

## Subnets
18. `snet-prod-hr-frc-001` – Primary subnet in prod HR VNet
19. `snet-dev-portal-weu-001` – Primary subnet in dev portal VNet
20. `snet-sbx-ai-frc-001` – Sandbox AI subnet
